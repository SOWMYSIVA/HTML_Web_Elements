Butterscotch Cake

Introduction

Delicious scratch-made Butterscotch Cake features super moist and tender brown sugar cake layers, filled with thick homemade butterscotch sauce, all finished with butterscotch buttercream. The perfect fall cake!

Butterscotch is a con fection made from brown sugar and butter, often with added corn syrup, cream, and vanilla, creating a rich, caramelized flavor. It is a versatile ingredient used in candies, baked goods, ice creams, and sauces. The name's origin is uncertain, but the confection is distinct for its rich, amber color and sweet taste.
